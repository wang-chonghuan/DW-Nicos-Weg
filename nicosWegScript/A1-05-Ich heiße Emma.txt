**LISA:**
**Ich heiße Nico.**  
**Translation:** My name is Nico.  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Ich"** means "I".
- **"heiße"** is from the verb "heißen," meaning "to be called" or "to be named".
- This sentence means "I am called Nico" or "My name is Nico."

**EMMA:**
**Hallo, Nico.**  
**Translation:** Hello, Nico.  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Hallo"** means "hello".
- Just greeting Nico.

**LISA:**
**Hallo. Also: Hallo, wie heißt du?**  
**Translation:** Hello. So: Hello, what’s your name?  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Also"** means "so" or "thus," often used to transition or clarify.
- **"Wie heißt du?"** means "What’s your name?"
  - **"Wie"** means "how" or "what".
  - **"heißt"** is the verb form for "du" from "heißen".
  - **"du"** means "you".

**NICO:**
**Hallo, ich heiße Nico. Und wie heißt du?**  
**Translation:** Hello, my name is Nico. And what’s your name?  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Und"** means "and".
- **"Wie heißt du?"** repeats the question "What’s your name?"

**LISA:**
**Ich heiße Lisa.**  
**Translation:** My name is Lisa.  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Ich heiße Lisa"** means "I am called Lisa."

**EMMA:**
**Nein, du heißt Nico.**  
**Translation:** No, your name is Nico.  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Nein"** means "no".
- **"du heißt Nico"** means "you are called Nico".

**LISA:**
**Das ist Emma. Emma spinnt gerade.**  
**Translation:** That is Emma. Emma is acting crazy right now.  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Das ist"** means "That is".
- **"Emma spinnt gerade"** means "Emma is acting crazy right now".
  - **"spinnt"** is from "spinnen," which can mean "to spin" or colloquially "to be crazy".
  - **"gerade"** means "right now".

**EMMA:**
**Ich … heiße … Ey!**  
**Translation:** I ... am called ... Hey!  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Ich heiße"** means "I am called".
- **"Ey!"** is an exclamation similar to "Hey!"

**LISA:**
**Emma!**  
**Translation:** Emma!  
**Explanation:** 
- Just calling Emma’s name.

**EMMA:**
**Aber jetzt ist das „N“ weg! Das war Nico!**  
**Translation:** But now the “N” is gone! That was Nico!  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Aber"** means "but".
- **"jetzt"** means "now".
- **"ist das 'N' weg"** means "the 'N' is gone".
  - **"weg"** means "gone".
- **"Das war Nico"** means "That was Nico".
  - **"war"** is the past tense of "sein" (to be).

**NICO:**
**Ich?**  
**Translation:** Me?  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Ich"** means "I" or "me" in this context, showing confusion.

**LISA:**
**Moment bitte. Ich muss noch kurz tanken.**  
**Translation:** One moment, please. I still need to refuel briefly.  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Moment bitte"** means "One moment, please".
- **"Ich muss noch kurz tanken"** means "I still need to refuel briefly".
  - **"muss"** is from "müssen," meaning "must" or "have to".
  - **"noch"** means "still" or "yet".
  - **"kurz"** means "briefly".
  - **"tanken"** means "to refuel".

**NICO:**
**Taxi! Stopp!**  
**Translation:** Taxi! Stop!  
**Explanation:** 
- **"Stopp!"** is a command to stop, similar to "stop" in English.
